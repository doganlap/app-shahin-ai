## üá∏üá¶ KSA-Specific Considerations

### Local Regulatory Frameworks
When selecting a GRC vendor for Saudi Arabia, ensure support for:

1. **NCA ECC** - National Cybersecurity Authority Essential Cybersecurity Controls
2. **SAMA CSF** - Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority Cyber Security Framework
3. **PDPL** - Personal Data Protection Law (Saudi Arabia's privacy law)
4. **CITC** - Communications & IT Commission regulations
5. **ZATCA** - Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority compliance
6. **CMA** - Capital Market Authority regulations (for financial sector)
